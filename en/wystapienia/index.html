Speaking & Lectures

25+ years of experience in strategic procurement and digital transformation — on the conference stage, in the lecture hall and at the workshop table. I share hands-on knowledge backed by the delivery of over 100 projects for organisations across the CEE region.

Speaking topics

Every talk I give is grounded in real projects and market data. I tailor topics to the audience — from C-suite executives to procurement specialists and MBA students.

  • Transforming the procurement function — from operations to strategy. How to reshape a purchasing organisation that has outgrown its traditional operating model.
  • SAP Ariba in practice — implementation best practices. Lessons learned from projects for ORLEN, KGHM, Żabka and PLL LOT.
  • Digital procurement in Central & Eastern Europe. The CEE perspective — digital maturity, regulatory landscape, supply chains and cultural challenges.
  • Public procurement meets technology. How to reconcile formal tendering requirements with modern sourcing platforms and process automation.
  • Building a procurement function from scratch. Strategy, structure, processes, tools — a roadmap for organisations setting up their purchasing department.
  • Procurement leadership — managing teams and driving change. How to develop capabilities, motivate specialists and steer transformation within the purchasing function.

For organisers

I work with organisers of industry conferences, procurement forums, corporate events and in-house training programmes. I offer flexible formats tailored to your agenda.

Keynote

A main-stage presentation (30–60 min) with multimedia support. Ideal for opening a conference or a thematic session on procurement and technology.

Panel discussion

Participation as a moderator or panellist in an expert panel. Discussion informed by hands-on project experience across industries and organisation sizes.

Workshop

Interactive workshops (2–4 h) for smaller groups. Hands-on work with case studies, process analysis and procurement solution design.

  • Languages: Polish, English, German
  • Format: on-site or online
  • Materials: slide deck, handouts, recording (by arrangement)

For universities

I bridge business practice and academic teaching. An alumnus of SGH Warsaw School of Economics, Wirtschaftsuniversität Wien and the Warsaw Executive MBA programme (University of Minnesota), I partner with universities that want to enrich their curriculum with insights from 25 years in the field.

Guest lectures

One-off lectures or a series of sessions within existing courses. Topics include procurement, supply chain management, digital transformation and SAP in practice.

Semester course

A full course (30 h) covering procurement strategy, digital tools, supplier management and negotiation. The syllabus draws on real case studies from the CEE market.

MBA module

An intensive module (8–16 h) for MBA and postgraduate programmes. Procurement as a strategic function, digital transformation and purchasing leadership.

  • Programmes: management, logistics, supply chain, economics, MBA
  • Format: lectures, case studies, group projects, negotiation simulations
  • Language: Polish or English
