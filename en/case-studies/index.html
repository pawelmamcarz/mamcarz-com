Selected projects

ORLEN CONNECT — the largest sourcing platform in Poland

Design and deployment of a centralised SAP SRM/MM procurement platform for ORLEN Group, spanning 15 entities across 4 countries. The project standardised sourcing processes, integrated with ERP systems and trained a 60-strong procurement team. The result was the largest sourcing platform in Poland, covering the full procurement cycle from requisition to contract.

4 Countries

Retail / FMCG

Żabka Polska — SAP Ariba for a 12,000+ store network

SAP Ariba Buying and Supplier Risk deployment for the largest convenience-store chain in Central Europe. The project digitised indirect-category purchasing, automated the supplier risk-assessment process and integrated with the existing SAP landscape. The solution enabled procurement centralisation for a rapidly expanding franchise network.

12 000+ Stores
2 Ariba modules
Mining / Resources

KGHM — SAP Ariba, Fieldglass & S/4HANA integration

A comprehensive procurement transformation for one of the world's three largest copper producers. The deployment covered SAP Ariba (sourcing and supplier management), SAP Fieldglass (external services) and full integration with S/4HANA. The project addressed the specific needs of the mining sector — from framework-agreement management to compliance with mining regulations.

Top 3 Global copper producer
3 Integrated SAP platforms
Aviation / Transport

PLL LOT — SAP Ariba for Poland's flag carrier

SAP Ariba deployment for LOT Polish Airlines — Poland's flag carrier with over 90 years of history. The project digitised procurement processes, rolled out sourcing modules and integrated the platform with airline operational systems. A key challenge was the aviation sector's regulatory landscape, including safety requirements and supplier certification.

90+ Years of LOT history
SAP Ariba Procurement platform
Energy / Refining

Motor Oil Hellas — SAP Ariba in the Greek energy sector

SAP Ariba deployment for one of Greece's largest energy groups — a cross-border project delivered with a CEE/SEE team. Motor Oil Hellas operates a refinery with a throughput of over 180,000 barrels per day. The project covered sourcing modules and supplier management, tailored to the Greek market and Mediterranean supply chain.
